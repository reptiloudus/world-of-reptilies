Popular Reptile Species

Chameleon

Chameleons are known for their color-changing abilities and their long, sticky tongues used to catch prey.

Green Tree Python

This python is arboreal, vibrant green, and often found in rainforest canopies in New Guinea and Australia.

Leopard Gecko

Leopard geckos are popular pets, known for their spotted patterns and gentle nature.

Habitats & Lives

Reptiles inhabit diverse environments, from deserts and rainforests to grasslands and wetlands. Their adaptations help them survive in these varied conditions.

  • Desert Reptiles: Adapted to hot, arid environments.
  • Rainforest Reptiles: Thrive in humid, lush forests.
  • Grassland Reptiles: Survive in open plains with seasonal changes.

Conservation & Protection

Many reptile species face threats from habitat loss, climate change, and illegal trade. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserve their diversity and ensure their survival.
