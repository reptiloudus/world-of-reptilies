Discover the Amazing Lizards

Lizards are a diverse group of reptiles found in many habitats across the world. From tiny geckos to large monitor lizards, explore their fascinating lives and the efforts to protect them.

Popular Lizard Species

Komodo Dragon

The largest living lizard, native to Indonesia, known for its powerful build and hunting prowess.

Habitats & Lives

Lizards inhabit a wide range of environments, including forests, deserts, grasslands, and urban areas. Their adaptations allow them to thrive in both terrestrial and arboreal settings.

  • Forests: Dense canopies and understories.
  • Deserts: Arid regions with extreme temperatures.
  • Grasslands: Open spaces rich in insects and plants.
  • Urban areas: Gardens, parks, and city streets.

Conservation & Challenges

Many lizard species face threats from habitat destruction, the pet trade, invasive species, and climate change. Conservation efforts focus on habitat preservation, captive breeding, and public education.
